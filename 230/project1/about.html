About

What's this tutorial about?

This tutorial gives some suggestions about how to draw a map for a fictional world and how to derive information for worldbuilding from your new map. I made it with a high fantasy setting in mind, but it should work for other kinds of settings as well.

Why this theme?

My biggest passion and pasttime is writing high fantasy. As such, I've had to do a ton of worldbuilding. It's a really fun thing to do, so why not do it and homework at the same time? A few years ago I found this method by Holly Lisle that really helped me out, and thought I might practice it some more and share it.

Above and beyond
