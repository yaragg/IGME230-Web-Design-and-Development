This unnamed project was a three-person team project for a Game Design & Development course in the University of São Paulo.

It is a dungeon crawler with randomized maps, turn based battles and a plot inspired by Greek Mythology. It was developed entirely in RPG Maker VX Ace.

It can be downloaded here. It is in English and playable on Windows only.

Challenges

  • Time. It was a mid term project, so we had a little less than three months to make the game.
  • Team play. The other members didn't have much time to dedicate to the project, so we had to try to make up for it.

My part in it

  • Concept.
  • Story and writing. I came up with the main storyline, developed it and wrote the introduction as well as most of the cutscenes.
  • Soundtrack. I picked the soundtrack, using royalty free music found online as well as RPG Maker VX Ace assets.
  • Programming. I am experienced with RPG Maker in general, so I handled most of the events and scripts.
